  • Copper Alloys C11000 is available in strips, sheets, plates, round and square bars, seamless and welded pipes & tubes, Forged & buttweld Fittings, flanges, fastener, flat rolled bars, Wire & valves.

    Copper Alloys C11000 is used for electrical and electronic conductors, wave guides, cavity resonators, superconductor matrixes, vacuum tube and solid-state devices, and glass-to-metal seals.

  • Copper Alloys C11000 Chemical Data


    Copper 99.99 min
    Phosphorus 0.0003 max
    Remainder Total 0.004 max
    Tellurium 0.001 max
    Principal Design Features Copper, OFE, Oxygen Free Electronic. C11000 has conductivity 101% IACS, the highest among coppers.
    Copper Alloys C11000 Applications Used for electrical and electronic conductors, wave guides, cavity resonators, superconductor matrixes, vacuum tube and solid-state devices, and glass-to-metal seals.
    Copper Alloys C11000 Machinability The machinability rating of this alloy is 20. (Where Alloy 360 FC Brass is 100).
    Copper Alloys C11000 Forming This alloy has good ductility and may be readily formed by all conventional methods. Because the alloy is stronger than regular steel it requires more powerful equipment to accomplish forming. Heavy-duty lubricants should be used during cold forming. It is essential to thoroughly clean the part of all traces of lubricant after forming as embrittlement of the alloy may occur at high temperatures if lubricant is left on.
    Copper Alloys C11000 Welding Soldering is rated as "excellent", brazing is rated as "excellent", oxyacetylene welding is rated as "fair", gas shielded arc welding is rated as "good", coated metal arc welding is "not recommended", spot welding is "not recommended", seam welding is "not recommended" and butt welding is rated as "fair"
    Copper Alloys C11000 Forging The hot forgeability rating of this alloy is 65. (Forging brass = 100). The recommended hot working temperature for this alloy is between 1400 and 1600 F.
    Copper Alloys C11000 Hot Working This alloy's capacity for being hot formed is rated as "excellent".
    Copper Alloys C11000 Cold Working The capacity for cold working this alloy is rated as "excellent".
    Copper Alloys C11000 Annealing The annealing temperature for this alloy is between 700 and 1200 F.

    Density (lb / cu. in.) 0.323

    Melting Point (Deg F) 1981
    Thermal Conductivity 226
    Mean Coeff Thermal Expansion 9.4
