  • Principal Design Features This is a molybdenum alloy containing 0.5% titanium.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Applications Used for aerospace applications such as heat shields.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Machinability Machinable by conventional methods.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Forming The alloy may be bent, shaped or formed by conventional means.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Corrosion Resistance Good corrosion resistance to many acids, except hydrofluoric acid. Good in reducing atmospheres, but will oxidize in air at elevated temperatures.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Welding May be fusion or resistance welded. Note that at temperatures above 900 f the alloy will rapidly oxidize in air.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Heat Treatment Not applicable.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Forging No data.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Hot Working No data.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Cold Working May be cold worked by conventional methods.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Annealing Anneal in protective atmosphere at 2400 to 2600 F. Stress relief anneal at 2000 to 2200 F in protective atmosphere for one hour.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Aging Not applicable.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Tempering Not applicable.
    Molybdenum - Titanium Hardening Not applicable.
